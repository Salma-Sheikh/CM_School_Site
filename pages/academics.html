Academic Programs

Excellence in Education from Primary to Senior Secondary

Our Curriculum

CM Shri Public School follows the CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education) curriculum, providing a comprehensive and balanced education that prepares students for success in academics and life.

Primary Section (Classes I - V)

Age Group: 6-11 years

  • Foundation in core subjects: English, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies
  • Language development: Hindi and optional third language
  • Creative activities: Art, Music, Dance, and Craft
  • Physical Education and Yoga
  • Computer literacy programs
  • Value education and moral science

Middle School (Classes VI - VIII)

Age Group: 11-14 years

  • Advanced learning in Mathematics and Science
  • Social Science: History, Geography, Civics
  • Language proficiency: English, Hindi, Sanskrit
  • Computer Science and Information Technology
  • Introduction to coding and robotics
  • Sports and extracurricular activities
  • Life skills development programs

Secondary School (Classes IX - X)

Age Group: 14-16 years

  • Core subjects: English, Mathematics, Science, Social Science
  • Optional subjects: Hindi, Sanskrit, Computer Applications
  • Practical and laboratory work emphasis
  • Career guidance and counseling
  • Board examination preparation
  • Project-based learning
  • Personality development workshops

Senior Secondary (Classes XI - XII)

Age Group: 16-18 years

Available Streams:

Science Stream

Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics/Biology, English, Optional: Computer Science, Physical Education

Commerce Stream

Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, English, Optional: Mathematics, Computer Science

Humanities Stream

History, Political Science, Economics, English, Optional: Psychology, Sociology, Fine Arts

Assessment & Evaluation

We follow a continuous and comprehensive evaluation (CCE) system that includes:

  • Regular formative assessments and periodic tests
  • Project work and practical examinations
  • Oral presentations and group discussions
  • Co-scholastic activities evaluation
  • Parent-teacher meetings for progress review
